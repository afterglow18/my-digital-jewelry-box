Do I have to have this setup for Apple review or can they check it without me having an acct
You don't need to set up anything for Apple's reviewers — they have their own internal testing tools and accounts to verify in-app purchases. You don't need to provide them a sandbox account.

The Sandbox Tester is only for your own testing before/after submission so you can verify purchases work without spending real money.

For Apple's review:

Just make sure your IAPs are submitted alongside the app binary ✅ (which they are)
No test credentials needed since your app has no login
For your own testing in TestFlight:

Set up a Sandbox Tester account in App Store Connect → Users & Access → Sandbox Testers
Sign into it on your iPhone via Settings → App Store
That lets you test purchases for free before the app goes live 🙌
Ok the only changes I made were minor but the app now is perfect, should I stop the waiting for review and resubmit?
It depends on where you are in the queue:

Cancel and resubmit if:

You submitted recently (less than 24–48 hours ago)
The review hasn't started yet (still shows "Waiting for Review")
The improvements are meaningful — the purchase error fix, the hero image intro, and the autoFocus photo bug fix are all things real users will notice. Worth having in v1.0.

Let it ride if:

It's been 2+ days and approval could come any hour
Status has changed to "In Review"
In that case, let v1.0.0 get approved, manually release it, then quickly push v1.0.1 with all the polish. Apple's update reviews are usually faster than first submissions.
